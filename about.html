About:

Hello, My name is Evan Young. I am 22 years old and currently living in Denver, CO. I am enrolled in the class g102 web development immersive at Galvanize Platte Campus. Before this I have bounced around doing various odd jobs while doing as much traveling as possible. The idea of becoming a web developer first came to me during my first major adventure in Juneau, Alaska.


I had bought a one way ticket and endured 5 months of life-changing adventure and wanted to have more similar travel exoeriences while documenting it all through a travel blog. After extensive research I came across a popular traveler movement known as "digital nomadization" which can be summarized as the basic idea of doing online work while living remotely and being location independent. As soon as I came across this movement I knew it would be something that I would be into and now I am building up the necessary skills to eventually make the dream come true and live a sustainable travel lifestyle.
